Purpose

The purpose of this website is to be a welcoming place for everyone and anyone with a newfound love for Aerospace and Astronomy. Read my blog where I attempt to answer the grand questions of the universe and write about the latest advancements in fields of astrophysics and space exploration. Check out Astro Picture of the Week to catch a glimpse of the extraordinary beauty of deep space that envelopes our world. And explore the astounding history of space exploration ranging from the Apollo Missions to the Space Shuttle Program and beyond. Unveiling the Blue is a place representing everything Space and Astronomy has to offer. So have fun, learn something new and be sure to spread the awareness about astronomy wherever you can.
